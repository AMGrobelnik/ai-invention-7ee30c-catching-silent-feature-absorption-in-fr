=== (A) GATED / CONDITIONAL ACTIVATION-EDITING: VERDICT = PRIOR ART ===
Gating a dense edit by a sparse/threshold detector is established prior art, and the exact gated-dense operator the paper uses as a control is already published. Four cites anchor this. CAST [1][2] (ICLR 2025 Spotlight) compares the prompt's hidden state to a learned 'condition vector' and uses the similarity against a threshold as a SWITCH that applies a steering/refusal vector only when the input matches a target category ('if input is about hate speech, then refuse') [1]; the condition vector is fit on LABELED example sets. GUARD-IT [3] (preprint, May 2026) routes each query through a Sentence-Transformer Similarity Gate K(x)={j: sim(c_j, phi(x)) >= T} over clusters of the LABELED forget corpus and applies a norm-preserving rotation h'=(h - a*vhat(x))*||h||/||h - a*vhat(x)|| only when a cluster fires; the paper states 'retain preservation is a structural consequence of the conditional gate' [3]. GSS [4] (preprint, Feb 2026) publishes ESSENTIALLY THE EXACT OPERATOR the CCRG control proposes: a probe u monitors |u^T h| and, when it exceeds threshold eps, subtracts a steer v -- h' = h - G(|u^T h| > eps) * v (Eq. 3), multi-component h' = h - a_k SUM_k 1(|u*_k^T h| > eps_k) v*_k (Eq. 14); the probe u and steer v are OPTIMIZED from a 'memorization matrix' over 1,000 memorization-labeled sequences and eps is tuned to the 95th percentile of |<h,u>| on a validation set [4]. SADI [5] (ICLR 2025 Poster) builds a per-input binary intervention mask from contrastive-pair activation differences. GUARD-IT's own Related Work confirms the lineage verbatim: 'CAST condition the intervention per token using the alignment between internal hidden states and a learned condition vector, and SADI selects steering dimensions via contrastive activation masks. GUARD-IT extends input-conditional steering to unlearning' [3]. PID-style controllers [6] and Dynamic Activation Composition [7] further show the input-conditional-steering space is crowded. CORRECTION to the plan: the |u^T h|>eps token-local formula and the selectivity/decoupling/optimality framing belong to GSS (2602.08901), NOT to GUARD-IT (2605.12765, whose gate is a prompt-embedding similarity gate + rotation). Both remain valid prior art for 'gate a dense edit by a thresholded detector at matched footprint.'

CONSEQUENCE: the gated-dense control DENSE-SUB-ABL-GATED is NOT a novel operator and the SAE-specific value cannot be the gating idea. The distinction that remains is WHERE the gate comes from: every prior conditional method SUPERVISES the gate (CAST's condition vector fit on labeled sets [1]; GUARD-IT's labeled forget clusters [3]; GSS's memorization-optimized probe with tuned eps [4]; SADI's labeled contrastive mask [5]).
